Lesson 8: Function within a For Loop

In the seventh lesson, we reworked our code into a reusable function. This made it super easy to draw multiple curve stitches with precisely defined variations.

In this lesson we are going to take that function and put it into another for loop. The for loop will repeat the same block of code and automatically vary the angle or rotation to 45 degree steps. This allows us to easily generate an 8-pointed star pattern.

We also positioned our curve stitches at the center of our canvas and made their size equal to half the canvas width.
